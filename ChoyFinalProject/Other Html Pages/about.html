About Me

Hello! I'm Aron Choy, a dedicated Graphic Designer with a strong background in creating compelling visual content. With a degree from Santa Monica College, my experience spans designing logos, flyers, animations, and much more for various campus events and local businesses. During my time at college, I worked on a diverse range of projects, from crafting graduation graphics and holiday visuals to developing eco-friendly product designs. This experience has honed my skills in blending creativity with functionality to deliver designs that not only look great but also serve a practical purpose.

My proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, Autodesk Maya, and other industry-standard tools allows me to specialize in 2D/3D animation, video editing, and typography. I am passionate about creating visually engaging and effective graphics that communicate clear messages and leave lasting impressions. Outside of my professional work, I enjoy exploring new design trends, participating in design challenges, and continuously improving my skills. I am also an avid follower of design blogs and communities, where I stay updated with the latest in design and technology.
